I was born at Syros in 1966. Here I graduated the High School in 1984. I lived in Patras for 10 years, initially being a student of the Dept. of Computer Engineering & Informatics (Patras University) and later working towards my PhD Thesis. During that time, I participated in research & development projects at Computer Technology Institute.

After a short break at the Greek Army, I returned to Hermoupolis in 1995. I work at Neorion Shipyards as head of the Information Systems department. Since 2000 I teach at the Dept. of Graphic Arts & System Design of the Aegean University.

In 1999 came my first daughter, Arista, and in 2005, Elpida.

My research interests span four main scientific areas:

  • databases
  • expert systems
  • hypermedia
  • educational software
  • software design
  • information systems
  • algorithms and data structures

If you are interested in any of those fields, you can check out the list of my publications.

I like teaching very much and I enjoy swimming at sea and playing rackets at the beach. Trekking has taken me to some beautiful greek mountain tops. Recently, I am becoming obsessed with organic cultivation (olive trees, grapes, tomatoes, beans, etc, etc) - call to exchange experiences, local practices or seeds. To learn the rest of my hobbies, you should meet me in person.
